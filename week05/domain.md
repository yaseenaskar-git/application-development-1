# Chosen Domain: Simple e-commerce (products & orders)

### what does the system do?
The e-commerce system allows clients (users) who are interested in personal training to view the detials of different personal training packages they are interested in, and purchase (order) the packages they like.

### Who uses the API?
The application programming interface will allow the clients (users) who want to purchase (order) the personal training packages to view the packages through the frontend application of the system.
